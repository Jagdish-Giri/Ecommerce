Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

At Ecommerce, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Account credentials
  • Communication preferences

Automatic Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Operating system
  • Cookies and tracking data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Sending order updates and notifications
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving our website and services
  • Marketing and promotional purposes (with consent)
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All payment information is encrypted using SSL technology.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ecommerce.com
